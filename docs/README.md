## 📊 METRIK DAN KPI PENTING

### KPI Utama yang Harus Dipahami

**1. NPF Ratio**
- **Definisi**: Persentase pembiayaan bermasalah
- **Rumus**: (NPF O/S / Total O/S) × 100%
- **Target**: < 5%
- **Halaman**: Executive Overview, Quality & Risk

**2. FDR (Financing to Deposit Ratio)**
- **Definisi**: Rasio pembiayaan terhadap DPK
- **Rumus**: (Total Pembiayaan / Total DPK) × 100%
- **Target**: 80-92%
- **Halaman**: Quality & Risk

**3. Coverage Ratio**
- **Definisi**: Rasio CKPN terhadap NPF
- **Rumus**: (Total CKPN / Total NPF) × 100%
- **Target**: ≥ 100%
- **Halaman**: Quality & Risk

**4. Repayment Rate**
- **Definisi**: Persentase pembayaran tepat waktu
- **Rumus**: (On-time Payment / Total Jatuh Tempo) × 100%
- **Target**: ≥ 90%
- **Halaman**: Repayment Rate

**5. Achievement Rate**
- **Definisi**: Persentase pencapaian target RBB
- **Rumus**: (Realisasi / Target) × 100%
- **Target**: ≥ 90%
- **Halaman**: Target RBB

## ⚠️ RED FLAGS YANG HARUS DIPERHATIKAN

### 🔴 Critical (Action Segera)
- NPF Ratio > 8%
- Coverage Ratio < 80%
- FDR > 100%
- Repayment Rate < 70%
- Kol 3-5 meningkat signifikan (>20% MoM)

### 🟡 Warning (Perlu Monitoring)
- NPF Ratio 5-8%
- Coverage Ratio 80-100%
- FDR 92-100%
- Repayment Rate 70-90%
- Kol 2 meningkat signifikan

### 🟢 Healthy (Maintain)
- NPF Ratio < 5%
- Coverage Ratio > 100%
- FDR 80-92%
- Repayment Rate > 90%
- Kol 1 dominan (>90%)
